New York

Visiting each and every place in New York is impossible. Instead onw should visit must-see places first like the Empire State Building, the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, the Metropolitan Museum of Art. Enjoy the fine dining at Upper West Side and indulge yourself in bohemian shops of the West Village.

Miami

Miami is known for its hot hot hot weather and it is not just because of the scorching nightlife but also it has heavy Latin influence and spicy salsa culture. enjoy the nightlife or indulge yourself in the fancy meal of the celebrity-owned restaurants. In the day, hit the beach of course and experience the mesmerizing colorful art deco architecture or grab a cuban sandwich in Little Havana.

Grand Canyon

Theodore Roosevelt rightly said - "the one great sight which every American should see." Which is why The Grand Canyon is on every traveler's bucket list. If hiking is not a cup of tea you can still enjoy the sceneic drive through the park on a rented bicycle. Get enthralled by the stunning views of The Bright Angel and South Kaibab.
